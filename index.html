My Favourite Hobby

SQUASH

I Like squash because its really fun to play and whenever I play it I feel like I am setting myself free. In squash we combine strategy, finesse, mental and physical stamina in a high intensity game. I don't know any other sport which provides the environment to be able to duke it out with your opponent in the way squash does.

And plus the befits of squash are very good. It sharpens your mental skills,it can be played indivisually or as a team,it keeps you healthy and active and like i said its super fun,once you get the hang of it !!!
